ABSTRACT

E-commerce platforms are increasingly vulnerable to logical attacks (Broken Access Control, Race Conditions) and marketplace fraud (Counterfeiting). Traditional defenses like WAFs and manual testing are insufficient against these real-time, business-logic threats. We propose an AI-integrated Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) Proxy that utilizes machine learning to monitor traffic, understand business context, and perform real-time anomaly detection to block threats before they reach the main server, effectively protecting integrity and preventing financial loss.

OBJECTIVE
  • Develop an AI anomaly detection model specifically tuned for e-commerce business logic.
  • Implement a non-intrusive MITM proxy architecture for real-time traffic inspection.
  • Demonstrate the system's ability to identify and mitigate Volumetric Fraud (Race Condition) , Privilege Escalation (BAC), etc instantly.
  • Handling the future challenges and further training the model.
PROBLEM
EXISTING SOLUTIONS

Companies heavily rely on:

  • Manual Testing: Internal penetration testing or bug bounty programs. (Slow, cannot stop attacks in real-time)
  • Traditional WAFs: Designed for common signature-based attacks (e.g., SQLi, XSS), but they are ineffective against logical attacks like BAC or Race Conditions.

Our solution differs by providing a context-aware AI layer that offers a true real-time preventative defense against these subtle logical flaws.
